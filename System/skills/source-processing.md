# Source Processing Procedure

An agent may read a user-selected clipping and draft a source note with title, URL, author, confidence, claims, tags, and suggested links. Keep the raw clipping in `Capture/` and keep the draft separate until the human approves any move or prose change.

Do not monitor `Capture/`, delete duplicates, or move source material to an archive automatically. Follow `System/PROTOCOL.md` for every consequential action.
